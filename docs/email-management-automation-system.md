# Email Management Automation System

## Overview
This workflow automates the sorting, categorization, and response handling of incoming emails.

---

## Problem
Managing large volumes of emails manually can be overwhelming, leading to missed messages and delayed responses.

---

## Solution
Built a system that:
- Automatically categorizes emails
- Triggers appropriate responses
- Logs email activity

---

## Workflow Breakdown
1. Email Trigger
2. AI/Logic-based Categorization
3. Response Automation
4. Data Logging

---

## Use Cases
- Executive assistants
- Customer support teams
- Business operations

---

## Industries
- Corporate Operations
- Customer Support
- Professional Services
- Startups

---

## Tools Used
- n8n
- Email APIs
- AI classification logic

---

## Business Impact
- Saves time
- Improves inbox organization
- Enhances response speed
- Reduces human error
