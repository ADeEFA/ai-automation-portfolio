# Cold Lead Generation System

## Overview
This workflow automates the identification, collection, and management of potential leads for outreach campaigns.

---

## Problem
Manual lead generation is time-consuming and inefficient, limiting business growth opportunities.

---

## Solution
Developed a system that:
- Identifies potential leads
- Organizes them into structured data
- Prepares them for outreach

---

## Workflow Breakdown
1. Data Source Trigger
2. Lead Extraction
3. Data Structuring
4. Storage for outreach

---

## Use Cases
- Sales teams
- Marketing agencies
- Freelancers and consultants

---

## Industries
- Sales
- Marketing
- SaaS
- Business Development

---

## Tools Used
- n8n
- APIs / scraping tools
- Google Sheets / CRM

---

## Business Impact
- Speeds up lead generation
- Improves targeting
- Supports scalable outreach
- Increases revenue potential
