# Real Estate Lead Onboarding System

## Overview
This workflow automates the process of capturing, organizing, and responding to real estate leads, ensuring no potential client is missed.

---

## Problem
Real estate businesses often handle multiple inquiries manually, leading to delayed responses, lost leads, and poor customer experience.

---

## Solution
Built an automated workflow that:
- Captures incoming leads
- Stores them in a structured system
- Triggers follow-up communication automatically

---

## Workflow Breakdown
1. Webhook Trigger – receives lead data
2. Data Storage – logs lead into system (e.g., Google Sheets/CRM)
3. Email Automation – sends confirmation or follow-up message
